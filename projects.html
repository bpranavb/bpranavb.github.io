PROJECT_MITO_BERT

MitoBERT Research

MitoBERT is a specialized research project conducted within the Northeastern University AI Club. The goal is to adapt BERT-style transformer architectures to the language of biology, specifically mitochondrial DNA. By treating DNA sequences as "text," we can leverage self-attention mechanisms to identify patterns and mutations that might be overlooked by traditional bioinformatics tools.

Beyond bioinformatics, I am developing a drone-based computer vision system for my robotics club. This project utilizes the YOLO model for real-time object detection combined with SLAM to create detailed 3D maps of environments. The objective is to create a drone that can navigate autonomously while "remembering" objects it has encountered.
